Motu Patlu is an Indian animated sitcom for kids and families that premiered on Nickelodeon India in 2012. The show is about two best friends, Motu and Patlu, who live in Furfuri Nagar and often get into trouble due to Motu's clumsiness. With the help of their friends, they eventually find their way out of these situations. The show is inspired by the characters of Lot Pot Comics and features genres like action, adventure, comedy, family, and fantasy.

Dora the Explorer taught children how to share, count, read and be accepting of others. Her lessons will last a lifetime for the children who grew up watching her because she allowed them to be part of her world and never talked down to them. Luigi is a character in Nintendo's Mario franchise, and Mario's younger fraternal twin brother and sidekick. He first appeared in the Mario Bros. arcade cabinet as the second player character, created by Gunpei Yokoi. In the western version of Super Mario Bros. 2 for the NES, Luigi was a playable character from the beginning, along with other characters who each had slightly different attributes. Luigi is often portrayed as a bit taller and more skittish than Mario.

The Boss Baby is a 2017 DreamWorks Animation film about a 7-year-old boy named Tim who becomes jealous when his parents give all their attention to his new baby brother, Boss Baby, who wears a suit and carries a briefcase. Tim discovers that Boss Baby can talk and is on a secret mission, so he reluctantly teams up with his baby brother for an adventure that could change the world. The film stars Alec Baldwin as Boss Baby and was directed by Tom McGrath. It also features over 60 actors and 31 characters, including Jimbo and Big Boss Baby.

The bald eagle has been the national emblem of the United States since 1782 and appears on its seal. The name "bald eagle" comes from the Old English word balde, which means "white".

Lions Also known as the "king of the jungle", lions are large, powerful cats that are second in size only to tigers. They are apex predators that sit at the top of the food chain in the wild. Lions are known for their strength, speed, and hunting abilities: Size: Lions are large cats with canine teeth that can grow up to seven centimeters long. Speed: Lions can run in short bursts at speeds of up to 50 miles per hour. Hunting: Lions are great hunters that hunt in packs and use different strategies. They can see better than humans in the dark, and will also hunt during storms when cloud cover and other conditions make it harder for prey to see or hear them.

Parrots are beautiful medium-sized birds that live in forests. Parrots are not only found in India but also in various warm countries. Parrots are known as talking birds because they can imitate the human voice. Parrots eat multiple grains, fruits and vegetables, boiled rice, etc.
